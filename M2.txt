11.impact economic env on marketing company product.
>>economic env of marketing 
>consist factor affecting consumer purchasing power & spending patterns.
>nation vary in their level & dis of income.some have subsistence economy & consume most of their own agricultural & industrial output.
>industrial economy offer rich market for diff kind of goods.
>change in income, cost of living, interest rate, borrowing pattern have large impact on market & company watch these by using economic forecasting.
>business dont have to be wiped out by economic downturn or caught short in boom with adequate warning they can take adv of changes in economic env.
>marketer must pay close attention to major trend & consumer spending patter both acroos & within world of market.
>>economic trends:
>income distribution
-income dis not equal in nepal as rich are becoming richer.
-due to remittance dis has been changing slowly.
>spending trend
-consumer having diff income lvl have diff spending habit.
>per capita income
-indicate purchasing power of market even within region there may be variation in per capita income.
>trade cycle
-prosperity
.high employment, low inflation, high income & purchasing power & create opp for marketer.
-recission
.high unemployment, low purchasing power and create challenge for marketer.
-depression
.high unemploy, low wages
-recovery
.gov use measure to control money supply, control saving & exp to reduce unemploy and increase purchasing power.
>willingness to spend
-depend on buying power,family background & economic condiditon.
-eg:income increase and gov subsidy increase then people make inv and willingness to spend increases.
>inflation
-fall in purchasing power of money caused by increase in circulation of money.
-war,political instability & natural disaster cause inflation due to increase money without supply of demand product & price increase.

19. marketing mix & how competitive env influence choice of marketing mix.
>marketing mix
-set of marketing element company uses to achieve its goals.
-include promotion,produce,price and place.
>>competitive env influence choice of marketing mix
>competitor type
-if diff good used to satisfy same need its common competition.
-if common good used to satisfy single need its composition competition.
-if to satisfy single need one brand used insted of another its brand competition.
>nature of competition
-can be oligopoly where seller are limited.
-eg: nvidia for h100 gpu for ai training.
-it also can pe pure competitive where there is more supplier.
-eg: samsung,google,huwawei,nothig etc for mobile.
>competition lvl
-include price & non price competition where price competition done by increasing or decreasing price and non price competition done showing diff btwn good,quality & feature.
